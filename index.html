Zero-to-One GTM Framework

The pattern is the same across every engagement: no entity, no local relationships, no commercial precedent, no team. Ferran builds all of it.

  • Market assessment — Validate whether the market makes sense before committing resources
  • Entity and operations — Incorporation, fiscal structuring, regulatory groundwork
  • First relationships — Building the network required to open doors with the right buyers
  • Lighthouse deal strategy — Identifying, targeting, and closing the first major customer that validates the market
  • GTM playbook — Building the repeatable commercial model: positioning, outbound, proposals, pricing
  • Team structure and handoff — Designing and hiring the team, then stepping back once traction is established

The engagement ends with a running business, not a deck.
